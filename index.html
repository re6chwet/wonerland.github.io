About

The $LAND platform announces a revolutionary era, allowing for an intricate mix of unique ownership structures and a new approach to value creation and sharing. Rooted deeply in blockchain technology’s principles, $LAND seamlessly combines off-chain functionality with the value of decentralized systems, offering a solution where influencers and businesses can mint their proprietary Wonderland. This allows audiences not just to engage but to stake a claim in their favorite entities’ growth and success.

Creation of Wonderland

At the heart of the $LAND platform is the ability for influencers and businesses to create their unique Wonderland
 
  • Tailored Design: Influencers/businesses can design the functional aspects of their Wonderland. They can choose the total number of Wonderland available and specific functionalities or rights attached to these Wonderland

Public Marketplace Dynamics

Once the Wonderland are created, they are introduced to a public marketplace.
 
  • Listing: Wonderland are listed with a detailed description, price, royalty, total quantity, and other relevant details
  • Purchase: Interested parties can buy these Wonderland using the ETH deposited in users’ accounts
  • Secondary Market: After the initial purchase, Wonderland can be traded, sold, or held, depending on the holder’s preferences. This provides liquidity and price discovery for the Wonderland

Engagement and Benefits

Holding a share is more than just symbolic ownership. It offers a range of benefits:
 
  • Exclusive Content & Access: Influencers might provide share owners with exclusive content, behind-the-scenes looks, early access to new releases and to private groups with them
  • Voting Rights: In some instances, especially for businesses, holding Wonderland might grant users the right to vote on specific decisions, just like shareholder voting in traditional stocks
  • Dividends & Rewards: Depending on the structure set by the influencer/business, Wonderland might entitle the holder to periodic dividends or rewards, which could be in the form of content, discounts, or even cryptocurrency

Integration with the $LAND Token

While individual Wonderland represent ownership in specific influencers or businesses, the $LAND  token acts as the universal medium of value within the ecosystem
 
  • Staking: Beyond the transactional utility, users can stake their $LAND tokens to earn rewards, gain premium platform features, or get priority access to new share launches
  • Revenue Sharing: The ecosystem is designed to reward $LAND holders. A part (75%) of the platform’s revenue is periodically distributed amongst token holders, proportional to their holdings

Integration with existing Platforms

Recognizing the potency of existing social platforms, for example twitter, $LAND will offer integrations allowing influencers to connect their profiles, enhancing the visibility of their Wonderland and broadening their reach
 
Meanwhile, for businesses, API integrations will be available, enabling seamless synchronization of their operations with the $LAND ecosystem, be it for analytics, CRM, or marketing endeavors

Tokenomics

Launched on August 18th, the $LAND token has a streamlined and transparent structure. Here are its key characteristics:

Supply Details

  • Both the circulating and maximum supply of the $LAND token is capped at 210,000 tokens

Liquidity Provision

  • To ensure trust and stability, the paired liquidity is locked for a duration of 365 days. Liquidity lock link can be found here

Ownership Renouncement

  • At the moment of deployment, ownership of the contract was renounced, indicating that no single entity has control over it

Contract Specifications

  • The $LAND token operates on a standard ERC-20 contract. In its commitment to simplicity and transparency, the contract does not feature any extra functionalities — there are no taxes, blacklists, or holding limits

Revenue Sharing & Generation

A sustainable revenue model is paramount for the prolonged success and growth of the $LAND platform. Ensuring fairness and transparency, our revenue model is designed to benefit both $LAND token holders and support the continuous development and promotion of the platform
 
$LAND Holders (75% of Revenue)
 
As a testament to our commitment to the community, a significant portion of the revenue, amounting to 75%, is directly allocated to $LAND token holders. This ensures that as the platform grows and prospers, so does the community that believes and invests in it. Revenue distribution to holders will be proportional to their token holdings, and it reinforces the idea that holding $LAND is not just a stake in the platform but also a share in its success.

Platform Development, Maintenance, and Marketing (25% of Revenue)

The remaining 25% is strategically reinvested into the platform. This allocation ensures:

Team Compensation

  • Rewarding the hard-working team behind $LAND, ensuring that we continue to attract and retain top talent

Maintenance

  • To guarantee a seamless user experience, continuous platform updates, and robust security measures

Marketing & Outreach

  • To grow the platform's user base, foster partnerships, and maintain a dominant presence in the industry
